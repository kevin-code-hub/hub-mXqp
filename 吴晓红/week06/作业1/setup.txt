1: 安装包下载地址：https://www.elastic.co/cn/downloads/elasticsearch
elasticsearch-9.3.0-windows-x86_64.zip
解压后需要修改es的配置文件elasticsearch.yml
# 添加或修改为以下配置
xpack.security.enabled: false
network.host: 0.0.0.0
discovery.seed_hosts: ["host1"]

elasticsearch-analysis-ik-9.3.0.zip下载后解压文件夹放在plugins文件夹中
plugins/

elasticsearch.bat
